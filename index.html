🎨 canvasx - Infinite workspace for your big ideas

Download CanvasX for Windows

CanvasX provides an open space for your thoughts. You can sketch, build diagrams, take notes, and write code on a single, infinite board. The tool organizes your work with hand-drawn shapes, sticky notes, and tables. It keeps your data private with end-to-end encryption while you collaborate with your team in real time.

✨ Core Features

  • Infinite Canvas: Expand your board as much as you need. Nothing limits your creativity.
  • Hand-Drawn Style: Give your diagrams a human touch with smart sketching tools.
  • Code Support: Include syntax-highlighted code blocks for your technical documentation.
  • Collaboration: Work with others on the same board without any setup delay.
  • Privacy: All your data stays protected through secure encryption methods.

🚀 Getting Started

Follow these steps to install the software on your Windows computer. This process takes only a few minutes.

  1. Visit the official download page.
  2. Look for the list of release files.
  3. Select the Windows installer file, which ends in .exe.
  4. Save the file to your desktop or downloads folder.
  5. Double-click the downloaded file to start the installation.
  6. Follow the prompts on the screen to finish the setup.
  7. Open CanvasX from your start menu or desktop shortcut.

💻 System Requirements

CanvasX runs on most modern desktop environments. Please ensure your computer meets these minimum standards for the best experience:

  • Operating System: Windows 10 or Windows 11.
  • Memory: At least 4GB of RAM.
  • Storage: 200MB of free hard drive space.
  • Internet: A stable connection for live collaboration features.

🛠 Using the Application

When the application opens, you see a blank board. Click the toolbar on the side to pick your tools. Use your mouse to draw or place objects on the canvas. Drag items to move them around the board. To zoom, use your mouse wheel or the trackpad. You can add text by clicking anywhere on the background. Changes save automatically, so you never lose your progress.

🔒 Privacy and Safety

Security remains a priority for CanvasX. The software uses encryption to protect your boards. No third-party servers view your content. Everything you create stays private to you and your chosen collaborators. You control who has access to your boards at all times.

📞 Support

If you encounter issues during installation, check the repository issues page for common fixes. You can also offer feedback to help improve the software. The community values your input and observations as you use the canvas for your projects.
